Section 03

What Stays on Your Device

The app saves a small amount of game progress data locally on your device only, using your phone's standard storage. This data is never transmitted anywhere.

Stored locally on your device:

  • Level progress and unlocked levels
  • High scores and star ratings per level
  • In-game coin balance (virtual currency only)
  • Sound and music on/off preferences
This data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app. We have no access to it. It is protected solely by your device's built-in security.

About In-Game Coins

The coin system in Vegetables Crush Mania uses virtual in-game currency only. No real money is involved. Coins are earned by playing and spent on in-game shuffles. All coin transactions happen entirely on your device with no external payment service.
